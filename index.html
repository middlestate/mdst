Who we are?

We are a design company that puts people (and animals) first

Our name reflects our belief that creativity and innovation doesn't have to come from Southern California or the Bay Area. There is imagination and creativity right here in the middle of our great state.

Our belief is that compelling design only happens when there is joy in the process, deep bonds with those who you are designing for and a drive to make the world a better place.
